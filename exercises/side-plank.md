Builds lateral core strength and stability. Complements regular planks.

## Instructions
1. Lie on side, elbow directly under shoulder
2. Stack feet or place top foot in front of bottom foot
3. Lift hips off ground to form straight line
4. Top arm can rest on hip or reach toward ceiling
5. Hold position without letting hips sag
6. Keep core engaged, glutes squeezed
7. Breathe normally

## Key Points
- Don't let hips sag toward ground
- Don't let hips rotate forward or back
- Elbow stays under shoulder
- Stack your body - shoulder over elbow over hip
- Focus on control, not duration

## Modifications
- **Easier**: Bend bottom knee and rest on it
- **Easier**: Support on hand instead of elbow
- **Harder**: Lift top leg
- **Harder**: Add hip dips

## Benefits
- Strengthens obliques (side abs)
- Improves balance
- Builds shoulder stability
- Functional core strength

## Progression
- Start with 15 seconds per side
- Add 5 seconds each week
- Goal: 30-45 seconds per side
- Focus on form over time
