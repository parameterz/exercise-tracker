Perfect warm-up and shoulder health exercise. Improves posture and counteracts hunching.

## Instructions
1. Hold band at chest height with arms extended
2. Hands shoulder-width apart (adjust for resistance)
3. Pull band apart by moving hands out to sides
4. Squeeze shoulder blades together at end
5. Keep arms straight throughout
6. Return to start with control

## Key Points
- Can use light or medium resistance band
- Focus on shoulder blade movement, not just arms
- Keep chest proud throughout
- No momentum - slow and controlled
- Should feel it between shoulder blades

## Benefits
- Strengthens upper back (rhomboids, rear delts)
- Improves posture
- Prepares shoulders for pressing movements
- Can do anywhere, anytime

## Variations
- High pull-aparts (above head height)
- Low pull-aparts (at waist height)
- Diagonal pull-aparts

## Perfect For
- Warm-up before upper body work
- Quick posture reset during the day
- Shoulder health maintenance
