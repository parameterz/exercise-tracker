Moderate-paced cardio for fat loss and aerobic conditioning.

## Instructions
- 10-20 minutes at conversation pace
- Should be able to talk but slightly breathless
- Maintain steady, consistent effort throughout
- Push AND pull with arms for full-body engagement

## Intensity Guidelines
- **RPE 6-7 out of 10** (Rate of Perceived Exertion)
- Could maintain this pace for 30+ minutes if needed
- Breathing elevated but controlled
- Can speak in full sentences

## Benefits
- Burns calories without excessive fatigue
- Improves aerobic fitness
- Active recovery option
- Joint-friendly cardio

## Tips
- Start with 10 minutes if new to cardio
- Add 2-3 minutes per week
- Perfect for recovery days or warm-up

## Progression
- Week 1-2: 10 minutes
- Week 3-4: 12 minutes
- Week 5-6: 15 minutes
- Week 7+: 15-20 minutes
